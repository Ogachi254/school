Our Programs

  • Primary Education: We offer a comprehensive primary education program that prepares students for secondary school. Our primary school program includes subjects such as math, science, English, social studies, and the arts.
  • Secondary Education: We offer a rigorous secondary education program that prepares students for college and beyond. Our secondary school program includes subjects such as AP classes, honors classes, and electives.
  • Extracurricular Activities: We offer a variety of extracurricular activities, including sports, clubs, and student government. Our extracurricular activities provide students with opportunities to develop their talents and interests.
